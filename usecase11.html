Drone Delivery Service

Use Case 11: Add Drone

When a drone is being registered on the system, its information is added by the system administrator. Such information would include: its load capasity, range, speed, and general information of the specs of the drone.

When a drone is being registered in the system, the drone must first excist and be ready for service. When being registered by the system admin using the Add Drone method, the drones information such as speed, capacity, range is taken in and registed on the database.
